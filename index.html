About Me

My name is Andy Hardy. I currently live in Frisco Colorado with my wife, Katie. We moved here from the Boston area in 2014, leaving our old jobs, and lack of snow, happily behind us. Above all, I like to spend time outside. I ski in the winter, I bike and run in the summer, and almost always I am thinking about when I am going to do one of those activities next. This is what brought me to Colorado!

I am a full stack web developer with experience building MERN stack web apps to solve real-world problems. Through the completion of the Denver University Coding Boot Camp, I am experienced developing full stack web apps, using JavaScript, HTML and CSS. I am also experienced developing within a group environment using agile methodology.

I am building upon 15 years experience in the sciences, as a chemist, where I thrived applying logic to solve problems on a daily basis. Through the years as a professional chemist, turned coder, I have refined my skills as a pragmatic, adaptable and flexible to change, clear and concise communicator. I am truly ecstatic about the possibility to leverage my problem solving, and communication skills toward success as a member of your team!
